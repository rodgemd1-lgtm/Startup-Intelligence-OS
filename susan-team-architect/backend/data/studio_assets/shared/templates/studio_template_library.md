# Studio Template Library
## Initial Reusable Scaffolds For Design And Marketing Studios

---

## Template 1: Landing Page Build Brief

Use for:
- acquisition surfaces
- flagship homepages
- launch pages

Required inputs:
- audience
- belief shift
- top objections
- proof assets
- moments of truth

Required sections:
1. Page objective
2. 5 Whys
3. JTBD
4. emotional arc
5. section blueprint
6. proof plan
7. motion plan
8. screenshot plan
9. experiment plan

---

## Template 2: App Flow Review

Use for:
- onboarding
- activation
- day-two return
- recovery flows

Required sections:
1. user state
2. immediate job
3. emotional state
4. moments of truth
5. friction points
6. reassurance points
7. progress visibility
8. experiment recommendation

---

## Template 3: Research Spine To Asset Cascade

Use for:
- decks
- memos
- white papers
- articles
- blog series

Flow:
1. research thesis
2. audience and resistance
3. proof stack
4. primary asset
5. derivative assets
6. screenshot or evidence needs
7. distribution or handoff plan

---

## Template 4: Executive Deck Skeleton

1. situation
2. problem
3. insight
4. proof burden
5. recommendation
6. next move

Rule:
- one claim worth remembering per slide

---

## Template 5: Strategic Memo Skeleton

1. decision
2. why now
3. evidence
4. options considered
5. recommendation
6. risks
7. next actions

---

## Template 6: White Paper Spine

1. category problem
2. why current alternatives fail
3. evidence and examples
4. workflow or operating implications
5. strategic recommendation
6. derivative-asset opportunities

---

## Template 7: Studio Critique Template

1. surface or asset
2. diagnosed job
3. what is strong
4. what is generic
5. what proof is missing
6. what should be amplified
7. what should be removed
8. what should become a reusable rule
