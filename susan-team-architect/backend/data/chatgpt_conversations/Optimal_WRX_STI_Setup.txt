Objective
Get a clean, punchy, non-boomy daily-driver setup for a 2018 WRX STI that favors front-stage clarity and solid bass without muddying vocals.
Key message
For a WRX STI, the best setup is usually:


let the front speakers do most of the work


keep rear speakers lighter/quieter


cross the sub low enough to avoid hearing it “from the trunk”


use time alignment / position for the driver seat


only use phase inversion if bass sounds weak around crossover


Best starting setup

Keeps midbass out of the doors where distortion starts

Quick tuning workflow

Then do this in order:

Reduce rears until vocals feel like they come from in front of you.


Only then touch EQ.
