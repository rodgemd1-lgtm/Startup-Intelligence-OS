# Moments of Truth in Startup UX and Landing Pages

Moments of truth are the points where the user silently decides whether this company understands them.

## Critical Moments

### First Fold
- Does the headline reflect the user's internal language?
- Is the first visual emotionally congruent with the message?
- Is the user told what this is, who it is for, and why it matters without cognitive drag?

### Proof Transfer
- Is evidence placed exactly where skepticism rises?
- Does proof reduce anxiety, or is it dumped as decorative social proof?

### Pricing Reveal
- Does price appear as a fair exchange, or as a surprise?
- Is value emotionally felt before price is shown?

### CTA Commitment
- Is the user emotionally ready for the ask?
- Does the CTA reduce risk and ambiguity?

### Post-Click Reassurance
- After a click, is there a calming micro-moment confirming the user made the right choice?

## Design Rule

For every primary journey, explicitly mark the trust moment, the hesitation moment, and the reassurance moment.
