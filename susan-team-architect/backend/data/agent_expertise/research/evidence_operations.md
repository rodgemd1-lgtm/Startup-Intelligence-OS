# Evidence Operations

Evidence operations turn raw sources into reusable institutional memory.

## Required metadata
- source_url
- source_type
- captured_at
- effective_date
- evidence_grade
- confidence
- verification_status

## Four-layer evidence model
1. Market intelligence
2. Clinical and operational workflow
3. Regulatory and enterprise constraints
4. Marketing and narrative assets

## Firecrawl usage
- Capture page markdown for retrieval.
- Capture screenshots for slide and messaging reference.
- Record why the page matters before ingesting it.
- Refresh screenshots when page narratives materially change.

## Quality gates
- Do not ingest derivative summaries when a primary page is available.
- Label official, analyst, media, community, and inferred evidence differently.
- Link every asset recommendation back to source evidence.
