# Recruiting Intelligence System

This pack defines how recruiting research and operations should work.

## Core Principle

Recruiting is a school-fit, coach-attention, and proof-distribution system.

## Research Layers

- official rules and eligibility
- recruiting calendars and timing
- coach communication and outreach
- film and highlight standards
- public visibility and social proof
- dashboard and pipeline operations

## Rules

- prefer official NCAA and NAIA sources for rules and timelines
- use Hudl and recruiting-platform sources for film and outreach workflows
- treat film, outreach, and social presence as one connected system
- store coach-response lessons and school-fit logic in memory
