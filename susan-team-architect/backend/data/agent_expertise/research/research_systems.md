# Research Systems

Research is a decision-support system, not a content-harvesting exercise.

## Core principles
- Scope the question before collecting sources.
- Separate durable facts from unstable claims.
- Prefer source ladders over source piles.
- Preserve provenance, contradiction, and freshness.

## Operator standards
- Every research brief should specify what is known, unknown, inferred, and unverified.
- Research outputs should map to a downstream decision, asset, or experiment.
- Contradiction is a first-class output.
- Refresh windows should match the volatility of the subject.

## Oracle Health emphasis
- Focus research on enterprise buyer reality, workflow friction, policy context, and implementation constraints.
- Treat screenshots, case studies, RFP language, and procurement cues as evidence.
- Build research packs that can feed slides, white papers, memos, and market narratives directly.
