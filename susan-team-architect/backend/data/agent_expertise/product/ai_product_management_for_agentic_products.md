# AI Product Management for Agentic Products

## Doctrine

- AI product management is rollout governance for non-deterministic systems.
- The core PM job is to reduce uncertainty, not to increase feature count.
- Evaluation, trust, and fallback behavior belong in the product spec from day one.

## Core Responsibilities

- define success metrics for conversational and adaptive systems
- coordinate sequencing across design, engineering, science, and evaluation
- define rollout gates for memory, adaptation, and automation features
- maintain the experiment registry for high-risk AI experiences

## Canonical Frameworks

- state-aware roadmap sequencing
- rollout by confidence band
- trust vs. capability matrix
- minimum viable intelligence
- evals before scale

## Key Questions

- what failure matters most to the user in this moment?
- what confidence threshold justifies shipping?
- what is the cheapest way to prove this coaching or adaptation behavior works?
- what guardrail prevents one good demo from becoming a bad product habit?
