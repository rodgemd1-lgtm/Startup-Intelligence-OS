# Slide Story Systems

Slides are strategic compression systems.

## Core doctrine
- One slide should do one job.
- Narrative tension matters more than template consistency.
- Evidence should be visible, not buried in speaker notes.
- Executive decks should move from context to tension to proof to decision.

## Slide architecture
- opener: why this matters now
- tension: what is broken or changing
- proof: evidence, benchmarks, screenshots, or workflows
- choice: what decision or bet follows
- next move: what should happen now

## Oracle Health usage
- Use workflow screenshots, interoperability diagrams, procurement language, and market comparisons.
- Show buyer stakes clearly: clinician burden, data fragmentation, enterprise risk, integration cost.
- Use Firecrawl screenshots to ground claims visually.
