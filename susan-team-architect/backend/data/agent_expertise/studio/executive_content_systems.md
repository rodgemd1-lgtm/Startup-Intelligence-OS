# Executive Content Systems

White papers, memos, articles, and blogs should all descend from one research spine.

## Asset hierarchy
- memo: decision and recommendation
- white paper: authority and structured argument
- article: insight and perspective
- blog: distribution-friendly applied point of view

## Production rules
- Research first, structure second, prose third.
- Every asset needs an explicit audience, decision, and proof stack.
- Reuse evidence, but rewrite framing for the audience.
- Do not let thought leadership drift away from source-backed claims.

## Oracle Health emphasis
- Anchor content in buyer pain, workflow truth, and enterprise implications.
- Translate complexity into operational clarity.
- Treat compliance and trust language as part of the writing system, not a late review.
