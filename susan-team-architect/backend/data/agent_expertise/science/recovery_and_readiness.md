# Recovery and Readiness

## Doctrine

Recovery signals inform decisions; they do not replace judgment.

## Required Coverage

- sleep architecture basics
- wearable caveats
- HRV interpretation limits
- readiness adjustment logic
- overtraining detection
- recovery interventions with evidence strength

## Failure Modes

- treating one readiness score as truth
- overprescribing based on noisy consumer wearable data
- confusing correlation with causation in recovery behavior
