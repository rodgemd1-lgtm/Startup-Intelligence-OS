# Exercise Science Doctrine

## Core Rule

Programming should adapt to the person, not force the person to adapt to the program.

## Required Coverage

- progression logic
- fatigue management
- deload logic
- contraindications
- substitutions
- population-specific adjustments
- evidence quality and uncertainty

## Current Priority Areas

- autoregulation and readiness-aware programming
- beginner progression with low intimidation
- older-adult training and balance/fall prevention
- injury-sensitive substitution logic
- GLP-1 muscle preservation contexts

## Unsafe Failure Modes

- generic overload prescriptions
- advanced exercise bias for beginners
- no recovery adjustment
- implying medical clearance where none exists
