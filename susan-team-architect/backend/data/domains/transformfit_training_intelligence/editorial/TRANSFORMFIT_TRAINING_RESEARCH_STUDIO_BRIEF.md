# TransformFit Training Research Studio Brief

The training-research studio exists to answer the question behind the workout, not just generate the workout itself.

## Core job

- turn ambiguous training questions into answerable evidence briefs
- combine open-access exercise science, structured exercise catalogs, competitor feedback, and user language
- separate what is proven, what is directional, and what is product judgment

## Priority question families

- How much volume is enough for hypertrophy without creating early fatigue debt?
- Where should autoregulation sit in a consumer fitness product?
- Which exercise substitutions preserve training intent when equipment is limited?
- How should TransformFit treat sleep, protein, and recovery signals in programming decisions?
- What do users hate about current AI or automated workout products?
- Which program shapes feel premium and coached instead of robotic?

## Research workflow

1. Frame the decision
2. Build the evidence ladder
3. Pull structured exercise and user-research inputs
4. Summarize contradictions explicitly
5. Translate the findings into program rules, product decisions, and experiments

## Output requirements

- question tree
- evidence ladder
- contradiction map
- recommendation with confidence
- implication for program logic
- implication for user experience
- cheapest next test
