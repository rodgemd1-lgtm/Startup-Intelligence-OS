# TransformFit Social Media Studio Brief

TransformFit needs a social-media studio because the brand must turn coaching quality and user progress into proof that travels.

## What the studio should solve

- build a repeatable short-form proof system
- translate workout progress and coach insights into social formats
- create reels, screenshot posts, and creator-ready assets
- connect public proof to product and landing-page trust

## Output expectations

- channel playbooks
- proof-post systems
- creator collaboration templates
- founder and coach content formats
- experiment logs for hooks and distribution
