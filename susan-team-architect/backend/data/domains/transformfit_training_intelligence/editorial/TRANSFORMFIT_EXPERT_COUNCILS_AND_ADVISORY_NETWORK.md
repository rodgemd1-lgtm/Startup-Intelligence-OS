# TransformFit Expert Councils and Advisory Network

TransformFit should not rely only on internal opinions. It needs recurring external pressure from science, coaching, product, and trust experts.

## Core Councils

### Human Performance Council

- strength and conditioning coaches
- exercise physiologists
- hypertrophy and performance specialists
- nutrition and recovery advisors

### Coaching Intelligence Council

- sports psychologists
- motivational interviewing practitioners
- coaching communication specialists
- relationship-memory and trust-design specialists

### Product Experience Council

- AI-native product designers
- workout logging UX specialists
- motion and systems designers
- accessibility specialists

### Algorithm Council

- applied data scientists
- recommendation-system engineers
- optimization and evaluation experts
- telemetry and experimentation leads

### Conversation Design Council

- conversation designers
- AI writing systems experts
- coaching prompt/evaluation specialists
- human-computer interaction researchers focused on dialogue

### AI Product Council

- AI product managers
- agent-ops and rollout specialists
- evaluation operators
- trust and product-risk reviewers

## External Source Targets

- top university labs in human performance, exercise science, and sports psychology
- public-health and evidence-review bodies
- elite coaches with practical programming credibility
- AI product and evaluation guidance from primary-source platform documentation

## Operating Cadence

- monthly: product, coaching, and experiment review
- monthly: new evidence and contradiction review
- quarterly: full program-system and coach-system refresh
- quarterly: role and org gap review

## What The Councils Should Produce

- evidence notes and contradiction calls
- updated guardrails and doctrine
- challenge memos on risky assumptions
- ranked recommendations for experiments and product changes

## Build Recommendation

TransformFit should start with lightweight advisory councils and beta coach groups, but each council needs:

- an explicit owner
- source priorities
- refresh cadence
- memory writeback
- adoption criteria for turning council advice into product rules
