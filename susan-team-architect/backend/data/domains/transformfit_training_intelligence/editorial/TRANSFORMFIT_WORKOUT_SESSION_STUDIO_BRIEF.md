# TransformFit Workout Session Studio Brief

TransformFit needs a dedicated workout-session studio because the live workout experience is now the product's most important trust surface.

## What the studio should solve

- Make workout logging faster than hesitation.
- Make the session feel coached without becoming a chat app.
- Separate stable structure from dynamic coaching.
- Build motion rules that clarify rather than distract.
- Treat the hardest set, adaptation moment, and finish as critical emotional states.
- Decide where personal context can show up without interrupting flow.

## Experience Model

- `session spine`: exercise, targets, prior numbers, rest timer, primary controls
- `coach thread`: short guidance, rationale, encouragement, and adaptation cues
- `state model`: warm-up, working set, hard-set recovery, adaptation, finish
- `relational layer`: contextual opening, bounded memory, and human-feeling post-session summary

## Output expectations

- journey maps
- state diagrams
- wireframes
- motion and haptic rules
- validation plan for gym-speed logging and coach usefulness
- relationship-safe zones and invisible-memory zones
