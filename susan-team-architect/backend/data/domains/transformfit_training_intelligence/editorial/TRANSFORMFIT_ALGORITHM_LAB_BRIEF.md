# TransformFit Algorithm Lab Brief

TransformFit needs an algorithm lab because adaptation, progression, and readiness should be explicit systems rather than hidden heuristics.

## What the lab should solve

- readiness and fatigue scoring
- session adaptation thresholds
- progression rule ladders
- substitution suggestions
- trust-preserving explanation of changes

## Output expectations

- decision trees
- scorecards
- telemetry specs
- evaluation harnesses
- fallback heuristics when signals are thin
