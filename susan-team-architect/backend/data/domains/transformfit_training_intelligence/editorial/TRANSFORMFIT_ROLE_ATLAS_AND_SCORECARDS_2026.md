# TransformFit Role Atlas and Scorecards 2026

This document defines the real role expectations behind the TransformFit build, including 10x signals and interview scorecards.

## CTO / Technical Co-Founder

### Owns

- system architecture across web, mobile, backend, and AI orchestration
- Supabase, FastAPI, LangGraph, streaming, and deployment decisions
- build-vs-buy tradeoffs for memory, retrieval, evaluation, and analytics

### 10x Signals

- has shipped a real AI product with real users
- has shipped at least one mobile app through the App Store
- understands stateful agent systems and not just LLM wrappers
- makes architecture choices that reduce six months of future rework

### Scorecard

- architecture depth: can explain state, memory, retrieval, and latency tradeoffs
- product judgment: understands why the workout session is the product core
- operational judgment: knows how to sequence build vs. launch risk
- execution proof: has shipped and maintained production systems

### Anti-Signals

- talks mostly in frameworks but has not shipped
- treats multi-agent design like prompt chaining
- ignores mobile, infra, or latency constraints

## Senior Full-Stack Engineer

### Owns

- workout-session UI implementation
- streaming token rendering
- GSAP and motion performance
- Capacitor and mobile polish

### 10x Signals

- can hit 60fps on complex animated interfaces
- understands why gym-state UX must stay stable and low-friction
- can diagnose streaming, React.lazy, and ScrollTrigger edge cases

### Scorecard

- frontend craft
- mobile pragmatism
- performance debugging
- interaction quality

## Product Designer (AI + Behavioral UX)

### Owns

- session flows
- coach thread expression
- onboarding and emotional journey maps
- trust language and uncertainty surfaces

### 10x Signals

- designs for ambiguity and AI uncertainty
- ties emotional design to user value, not visual novelty
- understands how coaching tone changes under fatigue, doubt, and missed goals

### Scorecard

- JTBD and moments-of-truth mapping
- system thinking across active and inactive states
- motion judgment
- trust and accessibility judgment

## Conversation Designer

### Owns

- coach persona architecture
- message taxonomy and silence rules
- motivational interviewing patterns
- fallback and escalation language
- voice differentiation across coach agents

### 10x Signals

- can make one coach feel distinct from another without gimmicks
- understands the difference between empathy and performative empathy
- can encode therapeutic-alliance logic into practical product rules

### Scorecard

- persona depth
- conversational restraint
- motivational interviewing competency
- trust-boundary awareness
- evaluation mindset

## AI Product Manager

### Owns

- non-deterministic product sequencing
- evaluation metrics for AI outputs
- rollout strategy for memory, adaptation, and interventions
- cross-functional prioritization between AI, product, and science

### 10x Signals

- treats AI product work as state, evals, and trust under uncertainty
- can define what “good” looks like before code ships
- can say no to features that increase ambiguity faster than value

### Scorecard

- roadmap sequencing
- evaluation literacy
- dependency management
- user value discipline

## Behavioral Scientist / Decision Scientist

### Owns

- adherence interventions
- recovery-from-failure flows
- ownership and identity mechanics
- behavioral experiment design

### 10x Signals

- turns research into sprint-sized interventions
- knows where persuasive design becomes manipulative
- reasons in emotional states and habit loops, not generic bias lists

### Scorecard

- experiment quality
- intervention realism
- ethical boundaries
- measurable outcome design
