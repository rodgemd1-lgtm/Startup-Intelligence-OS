# Founder User Research Operating Baseline

The foundry should not let companies build from intuition alone.

## Required user research outputs

- 5 Whys summary
- JTBD map
- moments-of-truth map
- objection map
- customer language pack
- contradiction summary

## Operating rule

Every new company or feature should answer:

- who is the user
- what job are they hiring this for
- what emotional and social jobs matter
- what proof they need to trust the solution
- what language they naturally use to describe the problem
