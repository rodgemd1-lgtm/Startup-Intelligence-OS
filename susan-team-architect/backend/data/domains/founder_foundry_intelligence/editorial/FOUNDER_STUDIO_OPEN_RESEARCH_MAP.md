# Founder Studio Open Research Map

The design and marketing studios should share an open-source research spine.

## Priority reference classes

- Apple HIG and platform design guidance
- WebKit and Chrome motion and transition primitives
- Double Diamond and discovery frameworks
- JTBD and customer-discovery references
- accessibility standards
- AI governance and trust references

## Studio usage rule

Studios should use open-source references for:

- design principles
- motion and interaction constraints
- trust-transfer patterns
- discovery and journey mapping
- critique criteria
