# Founder Launch Readiness Checklist

## Before launch

- product or asset has a named owner
- target user and success metric are explicit
- evidence used is current enough for the decision
- trust and compliance risks are reviewed
- accessibility and fallback states are reviewed
- instrumentation is in place
- support owner and response rules are assigned
- rollback or mitigation path exists

## During launch

- monitor operator scorecard
- watch leading indicators and trust exceptions
- log deviations from expected behavior
- capture user and customer-support signals

## After launch

- create decision-log entry if roadmap direction changes
- update experiment registry
- write postmortem or critique
- promote reusable lessons into studio memory or operating protocols
