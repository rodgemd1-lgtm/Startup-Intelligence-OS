# Founder Finance Operating Baseline

The foundry should give every company a finance operating layer before scale creates blind spots.

## Required finance artifacts

- budget guardrails
- KPI tree tied to cash and value creation
- pricing and packaging assumptions
- scenario model for base, upside, and downside
- operating review cadence

## Foundry rule

Ledger should be available to turn strategy and product plans into:

- unit economics logic
- budget constraints
- capital allocation choices
- launch and growth investment sequencing
