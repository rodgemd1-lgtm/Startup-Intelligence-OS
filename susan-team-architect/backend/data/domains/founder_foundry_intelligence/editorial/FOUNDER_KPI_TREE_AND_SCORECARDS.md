# Founder KPI Tree and Scorecards

## Purpose

The founder operator console should connect company metrics to interventions, not just reporting.

## Foundry-level KPI tree

### North-star

- number of companies that are stage-gate ready and operationally maintained

### Leading indicators

- execution tracks with owners
- companies with current evidence coverage
- companies with current trust baselines
- active experiments with defined owners
- launches with writeback completed

### Risk indicators

- stale data types
- unresolved contradictions
- companies without support plans
- experiments past stop date
- missing decision records on roadmap items

## Company-level scorecards

Every company scorecard should include:

- stage and current gate
- top 5 company KPIs
- trust and launch blockers
- experiment health
- operator alerts
- next actions

## Review cadence

- daily for active launches
- weekly for active companies
- monthly for dormant or early-stage companies
