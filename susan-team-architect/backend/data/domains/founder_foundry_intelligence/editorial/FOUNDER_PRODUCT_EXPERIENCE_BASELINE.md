# Founder Product Experience Baseline

The foundry should design for moments of truth, not just screens.

## Required design moves

- start with 5 Whys and JTBD
- map moments of truth and objection points
- define emotional outcome and proof requirements
- use motion to orient and clarify, not decorate
- design reduced-motion and accessibility fallbacks by default

## Foundry expectation

Every company should leave the foundry with:

- design principles
- journey maps
- moments-of-truth maps
- critique rubric
- production-ready output contract
