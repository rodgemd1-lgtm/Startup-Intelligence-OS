# Founder Support Operations Baseline

## Purpose

Support is part of the foundry because a company is not operational without intake, escalation, and feedback loops.

## Core support system

- intake channels
- severity definitions
- ownership routing
- response templates
- escalation rules
- incident linkage
- product feedback writeback

## Severity model

- sev1: trust, payment, safety, or complete outage
- sev2: blocked core workflow
- sev3: degraded experience with workaround
- sev4: cosmetic or low-frequency issue

## Required operating rules

- every sev1 and sev2 issue links to decision or incident review
- recurring support themes become user-research evidence
- support content should feed product, trust, and studio teams

## Founder dashboard requirements

- open issues by severity
- average response time
- average time to resolution
- top recurring themes
- unresolved trust-related tickets
