# Founder Decision Log Protocol

## Purpose

Every important company choice should become a reusable decision record so the foundry compounds instead of re-litigating the same issues.

## When a decision log entry is required

- stage-gate pass or fail
- pricing change
- ICP or positioning shift
- architecture choice
- launch approval
- trust or compliance exception
- experiment that changes roadmap direction

## Required fields

- decision id
- company id
- date
- owner
- decision summary
- context
- options considered
- chosen option
- why this option won
- evidence used
- risks accepted
- review date
- linked experiments

## Decision quality rubric

- clear problem framing
- evidence cited
- alternatives considered
- tradeoffs named
- downstream risks named
- owner and review date assigned

## Writeback rule

Every launch, experiment, incident, or strategic shift should link to one or more decision records.
