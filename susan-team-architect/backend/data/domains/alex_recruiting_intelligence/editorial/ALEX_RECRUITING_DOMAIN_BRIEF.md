# Alex Recruiting Domain Brief

Alex Recruiting is building a recruiting operating system around Jake's recruiting process.

## What the domain should solve

- identify the right schools and coaches
- create highlight reels and derivative proof clips
- build disciplined coach outreach
- use X/Twitter to create recruiting visibility
- track everything through a dashboard and recruiting board

## Success conditions

- the right coaches notice
- the right coaches respond
- film, outreach, and social support each other
- the family has operational clarity
