# Oracle Health Intelligence

This domain pack makes Oracle Health a first-class research, strategy, and marketing system inside Startup Intelligence OS.

## Four data layers
1. Market intelligence
2. Clinical and operational workflow
3. Regulatory and enterprise constraints
4. Marketing and narrative assets

## Studios
- Slide Studio
- Marketing Studio

## Intended outputs
- executive decks
- white papers
- strategic memos
- articles and blog series
- buyer narratives and enablement assets

## Operating principle
Research is the source of truth. Studio assets are derivative outputs built from evidence packs, screenshot captures, and buyer-language intelligence.
