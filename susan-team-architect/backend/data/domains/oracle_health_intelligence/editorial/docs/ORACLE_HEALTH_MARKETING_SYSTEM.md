# Oracle Health Marketing System

## Purpose
Build a world-class Oracle Health marketing department that can generate slides, white papers, memos, articles, blogs, and enablement assets from shared research.

## Department structure
- Oracle Health Marketing Lead
- Oracle Health Product Marketing
- Deck Studio
- White Paper Studio
- Memo Studio
- Article Studio
- Research Director
- Research Ops

## Methodology
1. Frame the audience and business question.
2. Pull relevant evidence from the four data layers.
3. Assemble screenshot and proof packs.
4. Choose asset format and narrative spine.
5. Draft the asset with explicit claim and source discipline.
6. Run compliance and messaging review.
7. Derive secondary assets from the same research spine.

## Example outputs
- CIO deck on Oracle Health interoperability risk and opportunity
- CMIO memo on workflow burden and operational fit
- White paper on enterprise healthcare data fragmentation
- Article series on implementation realities and buyer decision logic
