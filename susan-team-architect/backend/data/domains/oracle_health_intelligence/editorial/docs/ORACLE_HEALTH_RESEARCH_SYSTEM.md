# Oracle Health Research System

## Objective
Create a deep research layer for Oracle Health that can power strategy, product marketing, and executive content.

## Four-layer model
### 1. Market intelligence
- vendor positioning
- competitor narratives
- analyst and news shifts
- procurement signals

### 2. Clinical and operational workflow
- clinician burden
- implementation realities
- interoperability complexity
- workflow screenshots and claims

### 3. Regulatory and enterprise
- policy and interoperability mandates
- privacy and security constraints
- procurement and security-review requirements
- partner and ecosystem dependencies

### 4. Marketing and narrative
- buyer language
- objection patterns
- proof statements
- topic systems for content and enablement

## Research outputs
- evidence brief
- contradiction report
- screenshot pack
- buyer-language pack
- asset-ready proof library
