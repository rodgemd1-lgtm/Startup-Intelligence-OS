# Feeling Intelligence Methodology

Use this skill when the startup depends on trust, aspiration, motivation, emotional reassurance, premium perception, or landing-page conversion.

## Core Checks

1. **Feeling State** — What is the customer feeling when they arrive here?
2. **Message State** — What does the interface ask them to believe or do?
3. **Motion State** — Does pacing, reveal, and transition reinforce that emotion or fight it?
4. **Truth Moment** — Where does the customer decide to trust, commit, or bounce?
5. **Organic Signal** — Does the layout feel alive and human, or generic and mechanically optimized?

## When Susan Should Trigger It

- Landing pages convert poorly despite strong proof and product quality
- UI is clear but emotionally flat, sterile, or over-rational
- The startup targets anxious, skeptical, aspirational, or identity-sensitive users
- Brand perception matters as much as raw funnel efficiency
- The product needs to feel premium, safe, energizing, or reassuring

## What To Produce

- A touchpoint-by-touchpoint feeling map
- A moments-of-truth audit
- Motion narrative recommendations with restraint guidelines
- Layout changes that improve rhythm, emphasis, and emotional pacing
- Agent staffing recommendations across Marcus, Mira, Echo, and Prism
