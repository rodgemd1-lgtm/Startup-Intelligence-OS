Produce a deep research packet.

## Required sections
- research question
- scope boundaries
- canonical definitions
- methods and protocols
- source stack
- benchmark targets
- synthesis
- open unknowns
- recommended next research steps

## Rules
- be explicit about definitions
- prefer methods over vibes
- call out what is known vs unknown
