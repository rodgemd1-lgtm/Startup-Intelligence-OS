## MARKET PULSE

- **FDA unlocked RWE at scale (Dec 2025)**: The FDA removed the requirement to submit individual patient-level data in real-world evidence applications. This is the single biggest regulatory shift in a decade for Oracle's LHN asset — 129M de-identified records and 143 health systems are now directly monetizable for regulatory submissions. Pharma sponsors are beginning to operationalize this, but most haven't moved yet. Oracle needs to be the first vendor with a packaged offer.

- **Life sciences IT is a $69B market by 2034 on a 10.5% CAGR**: The clinical data management segment alone triples from $3.6B to $10.9B in the same window. M&A in life sciences hit $240B in 2025 — up 81% from 2024. Sponsors are consolidating vendors. Oracle needs to be the platform they consolidate on, not around.

- **Protocol automation is the highest-ROI AI use case in 2026**: Converting paper/PDF protocols to configured EDC databases is still a multi-month manual slog. AI is compressing this to days. Medidata and Veeva are building it. Oracle's Clinical One + AI Data Platform can build it too — but the clock is ticking. The sponsor who can configure a trial in 2 weeks instead of 3 months wins the 2026 enrollment race.
