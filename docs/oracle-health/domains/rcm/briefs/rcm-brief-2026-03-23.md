- **CMS prior auth rule is live.** As of January 1, 2026, payers must respond to urgent prior auth requests within 72 hours and standard requests within 7 days — or face compliance flags. The FHIR API mandate follows January 2027. Providers who don't automate PA inside their EHR workflow will be buried in manual rework. Oracle is one of the only EHR vendors with FHIR-native infrastructure to make this invisible to clinicians.

They just earned Black Book's #1 ranking in AI-driven RCM (Q1 2026) and are leading their narrative around "autonomous revenue cycle." They have payer connectivity at 1,000+ payers, 30,000 provider clients, and a 112% Net Revenue Retention rate. They are specifically targeting Oracle Health and Epic-installed accounts as the AI layer on top of the EHR. This is not a future risk. It is happening now.
