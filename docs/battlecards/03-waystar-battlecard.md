# BATTLECARD: Waystar
**Updated**: 2026-03-22 | **Threat Level**: HIGH | **Revenue**: $1.1B (FY2025, +17% YoY)

---

## QUICK DISMISS
"Waystar is strong RCM tech — but they're bolted on *after* the EHR. They can only fix claims *after* errors happen. We prevent the errors in the first place because we own the clinical data."

## WHEN WE WIN
- **Deal involves denial prevention or prior auth automation** — our AI acts on clinical data *before* the claim, not after
- **CMS-0057-F compliance is on their radar** — we're shipping now, Waystar is still building
- **They're tired of integration overhead** — bolting Waystar onto the EHR means maintaining two systems, two vendors, two support contracts

## WHEN WE LOSE — BE HONEST
- **Non-Oracle EHR shops** — Waystar is EHR-agnostic, we're not. If they run Epic or MEDITECH, Waystar plays and we don't
- **They want KLAS-ranked standalone RCM** — Waystar is 2025 KLAS top RCM tech vendor. We don't have an independent KLAS ranking yet
- **They want proven autonomous coding today** — Waystar acquired Iodine (AI CDI/coding). Our autonomous coding timeline is less defined

- "When Waystar flags a coding issue, how long does it take to get back into the EHR, fix the documentation, and resubmit?"
