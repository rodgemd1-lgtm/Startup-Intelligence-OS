## Stage-by-Stage Breakdown

### STAGE 1: MARKET INTELLIGENCE — "Know the landscape before the deal starts"

| SOP | What It Does | AI/Algorithm Capability |
|-----|-------------|------------------------|
| **SOP-33** AI Signal Detection | Continuously monitors competitive landscape and surfaces threats/opportunities before anyone asks | **SSA (Signal Scoring Algorithm)**: ML-based classification of signals by impact, relevance, time sensitivity. **Movement Prediction Model**: Pattern analysis predicts competitor's next moves from historical signals (e.g., 15 ML hires → product launch in 6-9 months) |
| **SOP-34** War Gaming & Simulation | Simulates competitive scenarios and pressure-tests Oracle Health strategies against modeled competitor responses | **Monte Carlo Scenario Modeling**: 10,000+ simulated competitive outcomes. **Strategic Positioning Optimizer**: Algorithmic approach to finding positioning that maximizes win probability across broadest deal scenarios. **Competitive Equilibrium Modeling**: Identifies stable vs unstable market positions |

**Value to Sales**: Before a deal even starts, Sales knows what competitors are doing, what they're likely to do next, and which positioning will work best.

---

### STAGE 2: DEAL PREPARATION — "Arm the rep before the call"

| SOP | What It Does | AI/Algorithm Capability |
|-----|-------------|------------------------|
| **SOP-08** Battlecard Creation | Produces one-page competitive intelligence assets per competitor with verified claims | **BES (Battlecard Effectiveness Score)**: Composite metric of usage + win rate delta + freshness + NPS. **Source Verification Protocol**: 100% of claims verified against primary sources. Confidence tagging: VERIFIED / INFERRED / ESTIMATED |
| **SOP-10** Pricing & Packaging Intel | Gives reps competitor pricing data and structural deal architecture guidance | **PGPS (Pricing Gap Priority Score)**: Algorithm that identifies which pricing gaps matter most for win rate. **Monte Carlo Discount Band Model**: Simulates expected discount ranges by deal size, competitor, and segment so reps know what to expect |

**Value to Sales**: Rep walks into every competitive deal with a battlecard that has verified intelligence, plus pricing data that tells them how to structurally architect a winning proposal.

---

### STAGE 3: DEAL EXECUTION — "Win the deal in the room"

| SOP | What It Does | AI/Algorithm Capability |
|-----|-------------|------------------------|
| **SOP-29** Deal-Specific Positioning | Produces custom intelligence packages tailored to THIS specific deal (competitor, size, care setting, buyer) | **Deal Intelligence Tiers**: Auto-triage by deal value/stage. Quick Brief (4hr), Standard (24hr), War Room (48hr must-win). **Cross-SOP Integration**: Pulls from battlecards (08), pricing (10), and win/loss patterns (09) to build deal-specific ammunition |
| **SOP-32** Predictive Deal Scoring | Scores active deals on win probability using CI-informed models, not CRM guesses | **DSA (Deal Scoring Algorithm)**: Multi-factor model combining competitive intensity + buyer behavior signals + deal structure fit + historical patterns. **Monte Carlo Win Probability**: P10/P50/P90 bands (not a single number). **CTI (Competitive Threat Index)**: Per-deal threat score. **Early Warning**: Triggers when deal probability drops below threshold |

**Value to Sales**: In the middle of a deal, Sales has custom positioning AND a predictive model telling them which deals are at risk and why — before it's too late to act.

---

### STAGE 4: DEAL DEFENSE — "Protect the win when attacked"

| SOP | What It Does | AI/Algorithm Capability |
|-----|-------------|------------------------|
| **SOP-12** Competitive Response Playbook | Detects competitive events and gets field-ready response to Sales in hours, not weeks | **CEIS (Competitive Event Impact Score)**: Predictive algorithm scoring event severity and pipeline exposure. **Response Timeline Optimization**: Monte Carlo model showing expected pipeline impact by response speed. **$2.1M-$6.8M protected pipeline** per major event with 24hr vs 1-week response |
| **SOP-30** GTM Artifact Factory | Produces scalable competitive content for marketing campaigns and sales training | **DES (Distribution Effectiveness Score)**: Measures whether intelligence was consumed and acted upon. **Quarterly Cadence**: Aligned with GTM calendar. **Weekly Competitive Flash**: Short-form intel digest to keep Sales current |

**Value to Sales**: When a competitor makes a move mid-deal, Sales has a coordinated response within hours. Between deals, they're getting a steady stream of competitive content that keeps them sharp.

---

### STAGE 5: POST-DEAL LEARNING — "Learn and improve from every outcome"

| SOP | What It Does | AI/Algorithm Capability |
|-----|-------------|------------------------|
| **SOP-09** Win/Loss Analysis | Systematically analyzes why Oracle Health wins and loses using behavioral science methodology | **Monte Carlo Deal Prediction Model**: Trained on interview data to predict outcomes. **Cognitive Interview Protocol**: Penetrates beneath stated reasons (price cited 62%, actual driver 18%). **44-Point Price Gap**: The biggest finding — stated reason matches actual driver only 36% of the time. **Revenue impact**: 15-30% increase from program insights |
| **SOP-31** CI Training & Onboarding | Scales the entire system from 1 person to 6/6 strategists | **Adoption Tracking**: Weekly login rates, usage patterns, self-serve rates. **Competency Scoring**: Measures time-to-independence for each strategist. **Hub-and-Spoke Model**: Strategists own production, M&CI owns quality. At scale: 6 battlecards/month capacity vs current 1-person bottleneck |

**Value to Sales**: Every deal outcome makes the system smarter. Win/loss data trains the prediction models, improves the battlecards, and refines the positioning. Training scales this from one person's capability to an organizational capability.
