# Beliefs

## Core
- Research first, always. No building without understanding.
- Agents > manual work. If a human is doing it repeatedly, an agent should own it.
- Plan before build. The process is: Research -> Plan -> Execute -> Lessons -> Docs.
- System over intelligence. A well-designed system with an average model beats a genius model with no scaffolding.
- Cross-domain patterns transfer. What works in one company often works in another.

## On AI
- AI agents are the most leveraged investment a solo founder can make.
- Context engineering matters more than prompt engineering.
- Memory without structure is noise. RAG without curation is hallucination fuel.
- The gap between "AI demo" and "AI in production" is 10x what most people think.
- Multi-agent orchestration is the right architecture for complex domains.

## On Business
- Founder time is the scarcest resource. Protect it above all else.
- Small teams with great tools beat large teams with bad process.
- Revenue comes from solving real problems, not from building cool tech.
- Dogfood everything. If Jake can't use it, customers can't either.

## On Life
- Family comes first. James, Jacob, Alex — everything else is second.
- Service matters. Army values don't expire.
- Be honest about what's not working. Admission is the first step to fixing it.
- Growth requires discomfort. If it's easy, you're not learning.
