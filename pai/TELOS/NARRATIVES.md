# Narratives

Personal stories and identity threads that shape how Mike thinks and operates.

## The Arc
Army vet -> Iowa State University -> Aurora Health -> Oracle Health -> AI founder

## Military Foundation
Served in the Army. Learned discipline, structured thinking, mission-first mentality, and the value of debriefing after every operation. These values didn't expire at discharge — they run the operating system.

## Corporate Chapter
Aurora Health and Oracle Health taught enterprise scale, healthcare complexity, and the gap between what technology CAN do and what organizations WILL adopt. Learned that change management is harder than engineering.

## The Pivot
Realized that AI agents could compress a 25-person team into a solo founder's toolkit. Started building instead of advising. The Startup Intelligence OS was born from frustration with how much founder time goes to operational overhead.

## Family First
Husband to James. Father to Jacob (15, football OL/DL, Shot Put, IMG camp) and Alex (12, soccer). Everything else — every company, every agent, every system — exists to create more time and energy for family, not less.

## Builder Identity
Not a manager. A builder. Happiest when shipping, debugging, or designing systems. Least happy in meetings that should have been documents.
