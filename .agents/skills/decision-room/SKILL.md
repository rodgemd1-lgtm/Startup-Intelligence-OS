---
name: decision-room
description: Use when the task requires framing a business or product decision, generating alternatives, comparing options, or producing a recommendation with assumptions and reversal criteria.
---

Turn the request into a structured decision artifact.

Required output:
- decision statement
- constraints
- options
- scoring
- recommendation
- assumptions
- reversal criteria
- next actions

If files exist under `.startup-os/decisions/`, update or create the relevant record.
