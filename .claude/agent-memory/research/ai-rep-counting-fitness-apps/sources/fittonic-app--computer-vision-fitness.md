# Fittonic: Computer Vision AI for Fitness Applications

- **Source:** https://fittonic.app/
- **Date fetched:** 2026-03-10

## Summary

Fittonic is a computer vision AI platform that provides real-time exercise tracking and feedback for fitness applications and live video streaming services. The platform offers automated rep counting, form analysis, and injury prevention by detecting incorrect movement patterns during workouts.

Key technical differentiators include edge-based video processing, meaning all AI inference runs on-device with no video data sent to the cloud. This addresses both privacy concerns and latency requirements for real-time feedback. The system delivers coaching through on-screen visual indicators and voice prompts during exercise sessions.

The platform supports gamification features such as leaderboards, achievements, and social sharing of workout results. Coaches can create custom exercise models by recording correct and incorrect repetitions, which Fittonic then uses to train neural networks that are deployed across subscriber applications.

Fittonic is available on iPhone, iPad, Android, and web browsers, with some features still in development. The platform is designed as a B2B tool that fitness app developers and streaming services can integrate into their own products rather than being a standalone consumer app.
