## Key Facts to Recall

### AI Tools — Current State (Q1 2026)
- Sora 2: best narrative/emotional depth, weaker on physical realism
- Veo 3.x (Google): best physical realism, production-ready
- Kling 2.6: first simultaneous audio-visual generation in single pass
- Runway Gen-4.5: best character consistency across shots
- Wonder Dynamics (now Autodesk Flow): acquired by Autodesk 2024; full CG character replacement

### Legal Landscape
- SAG-AFTRA 2023 TV/Theatrical: digital replica = requires explicit consent per use
- WGA 2023: AI output cannot be the "literary material" basis for a script
- US Copyright Office: AI-generated content without human authorship is not copyrightable
- ElevenLabs Eleven Music: only AI music tool with explicit cleared commercial licensing (Merlin + Kobalt) as of 2026

### Delivery Standards
- Netflix 4K HDR: ProRes 4444 or DNxHR; Rec.2020 PQ; Dolby Vision + Atmos required for Originals
- Theatrical DCP: JPEG-2000, 4K container, X'Y'Z' color space, Gamma 2.6
- ACES: scene-linear hub format; IDT → ACEScct (working) → ODT (output)

### Film School Framework
- AFI six domains: Directing, Producing, Cinematography, Editing, Production Design, Screenwriting
- NYU 2025: launched MPS in Virtual Production — first US film school VP degree
