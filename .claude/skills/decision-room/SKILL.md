---
name: decision-room
description: Use when the user needs a decision framed, alternatives generated, options compared, assumptions exposed, or a recommendation made.
---

Create a structured decision output.

## Required sections
- decision statement
- why this decision matters now
- constraints
- 3 or more options unless impossible
- option scoring across impact, effort, risk, confidence, and time-to-signal
- recommendation
- assumptions
- reversal criteria
- next actions

## Operating rules
- do not recommend a single path without alternatives
- do not hide assumptions
- prefer practical next steps over abstract theory
