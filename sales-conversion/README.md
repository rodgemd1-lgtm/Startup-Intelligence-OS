# Sales & Conversion — SaaS Sales Playbook

> Funnel optimization, CRM setup, demo strategy, and conversion processes from 0 → $10M.

---

## 1. Sales Motion by Stage

### Phase 1: Founder-Led Sales ($0-$500K ARR)
- **You** are the sales team — founders should sell first 50 customers
- No CRM needed yet (spreadsheet or Notion is fine)
- Goal: Learn objections, refine pitch, validate ICP
- Close rate target: 20-30% of qualified demos

### Phase 2: First Sales Hire ($500K-$2M ARR)
- Hire first AE (Account Executive) or SDR (Sales Dev Rep)
- Implement CRM (Attio, Close, or HubSpot)
- Document the sales playbook (pitch, objections, process)
- Close rate target: 15-25%

### Phase 3: Sales Team ($2M-$10M ARR)
- 2-3 AEs, 1-2 SDRs, 1 Sales Manager
- Formal pipeline management, forecasting
- Sales enablement content (decks, demos, battle cards)
- Close rate target: 20-30% for qualified pipeline

## 2. Sales Funnel Optimization

### Funnel Stages & Metrics
| Stage | Metric | Good Benchmark |
|-------|--------|---------------|
| **Visitor → Signup** | Conversion rate | 2-5% |
| **Signup → Activated** | Activation rate | 40-60% |
| **Activated → Trial** | Trial start rate | 20-40% |
| **Trial → Paid** | Trial conversion | 15-30% |
| **Paid → Expansion** | Expansion rate | 20-30% annually |

### Key Conversion Levers
1. **Reduce friction**: Fewer form fields, social login, instant value
2. **Time to value**: Get user to "aha moment" ASAP
3. **Onboarding**: Interactive tutorials, setup wizards, checklists
4. **Social proof**: Logos, testimonials, case studies on pricing page
5. **Urgency**: Limited-time offers, usage limits approaching
6. **Follow-up**: Behavioral email triggers, in-app messages

## 3. Demo Strategy

### The Perfect Demo Framework (15-20 min)
1. **Discovery (3 min)**: "What's your biggest challenge with [problem]?"
2. **Set agenda (1 min)**: "Here's what I'll show you"
3. **Tell, don't sell (8 min)**: Show their specific use case, not feature tour
4. **Social proof (2 min)**: "Company X had the same problem and saw Y result"
5. **Close (3 min)**: "Based on what you've seen, what would next steps look like?"

### Demo Tools
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Loom** | Async video demos |
| **Arcade** | Interactive product demos |
| **Navattic** | Self-serve product tours |
| **Calendly** | Demo scheduling |
| **Gong** / **Chorus** | Call recording + analysis |

## 4. CRM & Pipeline Management

### CRM Recommendations by Stage
| Stage | CRM | Why |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Pre-revenue | Notion / Spreadsheet | Free, flexible |
| $0-$500K | **Attio** | Modern, AI-native, fast |
| $0-$2M | **Close** | Built for startups, great UX |
| $500K-$5M | **HubSpot** | Free CRM + paid features |
| $2M-$10M+ | **Salesforce** | Enterprise-grade, integrations |

### Pipeline Stages
1. **Lead** — Marketing qualified, not yet contacted
2. **Contacted** — Outreach sent, awaiting response
3. **Discovery** — Initial call completed
4. **Demo** — Product demo scheduled/completed
5. **Proposal** — Pricing/proposal sent
6. **Negotiation** — Terms being discussed
7. **Closed Won** / **Closed Lost**

## 5. Objection Handling

### Top 10 SaaS Sales Objections
| Objection | Response Framework |
|-----------|-------------------|
| "Too expensive" | Focus on ROI: "What's the cost of NOT solving this?" |
| "We're using [competitor]" | "Many of our customers switched from X because..." |
| "Need to think about it" | "What specific concerns can I address right now?" |
| "No budget right now" | "When does your next budget cycle start? Let's plan for that." |
| "Need team buy-in" | "Can I join a call with your team to answer their questions?" |
| "Too complex" | "Most customers are up and running in [timeframe]" |
| "Security concerns" | Share SOC 2 report, security whitepaper, architecture diagram |
| "Can we get a pilot?" | "Absolutely — here's our 14-day pilot program" |
| "What if it doesn't work?" | "Here's our success guarantee / easy cancellation policy" |
| "We'll build it ourselves" | "What would that cost in eng time? Our customers find it's 10x faster" |

## 6. Sales Enablement Content

### Battle Cards (Per Competitor)
```
Competitor: [Name]
Their Positioning: [What they say]
Their Weakness: [Where they fall short]
Our Differentiator: [Why we're better for [ICP]]
Proof Points: [Customer stories, metrics]
Landmines: [Questions to ask that expose their weakness]
```

### Sales Content Library

- [ ] Security / compliance documentation
