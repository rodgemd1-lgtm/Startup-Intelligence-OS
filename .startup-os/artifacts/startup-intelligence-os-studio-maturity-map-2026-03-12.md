## Department Package Standard

Every department studio should ship with the same package shape:

1. Mission and scope
2. Intake and triage rules
3. Diagnosis protocols
4. Decision and execution templates
5. Evidence sources and canonical resources
6. Metrics and maturity scorecard
7. Memory and writeback rules
8. Escalation and handoff rules

## Six-Lens Maturity Overlay

Every department should be scored through the same six Susan lenses:

1. **Company Genome**: Is ownership, scope, and dependency structure explicit?
2. **Evidence Graph**: Can the department reason from current grounded evidence?
3. **Cognitive Studios**: Does it diagnose, generate options, critique, and produce?
4. **Trust and Governance**: Are permissions, accessibility, security, and claims covered?
5. **Operator Console**: Is the department visible and actionable from Jake's dashboard?
6. **Memory and Evaluation**: Does work compound through postmortems, experiments, and writeback?
