# Founder Decision Room Operating Pack

**Date:** 2026-03-12  
**Owner:** Jake  
**Company:** founder-intelligence-os

## Objective
Turn the Founder Decision Room into a repeatable operating system for framing choices, comparing options, recording decisions, and reviewing outcomes instead of relying on one-off strategy conversations.

## Operating loop
1. Intake the objective, stakes, and time horizon.
2. Frame the decision, options, assumptions, and reversal criteria.
3. Route evidence requests to supporting departments.
4. Write the decision record and linked execution sequence.
5. Review the outcome after execution and write back changes to the company genome.

## Required inputs
- objective
- stakes
- current state
- active company and project context
- linked evidence or source bundle

## Default outputs
- future-back option stack
- decision record
- execution sequence
- decision outcome review

## Review cadence
- Weekly: open strategic decisions and blockers
- Monthly: decision outcome review and reversal check
- Quarterly: future-back portfolio review

## Refresh and evaluation rules
- Every material decision must link to at least one evidence source and one downstream owner.
- Every high-stakes decision must receive an outcome review within one review cycle.
- If assumptions break, the decision must either be reversed or explicitly reaffirmed.
