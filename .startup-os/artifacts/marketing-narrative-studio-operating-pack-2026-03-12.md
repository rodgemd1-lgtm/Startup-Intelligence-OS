# Marketing & Narrative Studio Operating Pack

**Date:** 2026-03-12  
**Owner:** Jake  
**Company:** founder-intelligence-os

## Objective
Make Marketing & Narrative Studio an evidence-backed system for positioning, proof, asset production, and launch review rather than a loose copywriting surface.

## Operating loop
1. Intake the offer, audience, and launch objective.
2. Build the message map and proof spine.
3. Convert proof into reusable assets and experiments.
4. Review launch signal quality and proof density.
5. Write back winning narrative patterns and failed claims.

## Required inputs
- offer definition
- audience segment
- proof sources
- current launch objective

## Default outputs
- message map
- proof spine
- campaign brief
- launch readout

## Review cadence
- Weekly: message and asset review
- Per launch: campaign readout and claim audit
- Monthly: proof-density review and narrative refresh

## Refresh and evaluation rules
- Every major narrative must trace to named proof.
- Every launch must receive a readout with signal quality and reuse notes.
- Weak or unsupported claims must be removed from the reusable proof spine.
