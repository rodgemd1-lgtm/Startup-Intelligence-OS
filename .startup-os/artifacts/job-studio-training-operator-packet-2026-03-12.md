## Quality Gates
1. Source grounding: the session uses named examples and evidence.
2. Behavioral transfer: the learner can apply the workflow, not only describe it.
3. Workflow clarity: the path from question to deliverable is explicit.
4. Confidence calibration: the learner knows when AI output is weak or incomplete.
5. Reuse quality: assets improve the future factory rather than staying one-off.
