# Revenue & Growth Studio Operating Pack

## Objective

Turn Revenue & Growth Studio into an operating system for ICP clarity, offer design, funnel planning, proof tracking, and experiment sequencing.

## Current Gap

The department exists, but it is still underpowered on reusable operating artifacts. It needs explicit packets for:

- growth scorecards
- offer and funnel briefs
- experiment queues
- weekly growth review
- proof-gap logging

## Operating System

### Core cadence

1. Weekly growth review
2. Offer and funnel diagnosis
3. Experiment queue refresh
4. Proof gap update
5. Monthly ICP and pipeline reset

### Required source stack

- department definition in `.startup-os/departments/revenue-growth-studio.yaml`
- live source manifest in `startup_os_revenue_growth_officials.yaml`
- buyer and channel context from active company/project artifacts
- message-map and proof-spine inputs from Marketing & Narrative Studio

### Required outputs

- growth system scorecard
- offer and funnel brief
- experiment queue
- weekly growth review note
- decision packet when pricing, wedge, or channel choices become strategic

## Scorecard

| Dimension | Question |
|---|---|
| ICP clarity | Do we know which buyer and why now? |
| Offer sharpness | Is the offer specific, differentiated, and outcome-linked? |
| Proof density | Is the motion supported by clear proof, not just claims? |
| Funnel readiness | Do we know stage definitions, conversion risks, and owner actions? |
| Experiment discipline | Are tests sequenced, measurable, and learnable? |

## Stage Gates

### Gate 1: Growth brief quality
- ICP named
- offer named
- proof burden visible
- channel and stage assumptions explicit

### Gate 2: Experiment readiness
- metric target defined
- owner assigned
- time horizon defined
- failure condition explicit

### Gate 3: Strategic escalation
- pricing, wedge, or channel choice becomes a Founder Decision Room input

## Writeback

After every growth cycle, write back:

- what signal changed
- which proof gaps remain
- which experiments were promoted, killed, or repeated
- which messages or offers improved conversion
