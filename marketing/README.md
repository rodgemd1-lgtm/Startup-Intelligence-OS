# Marketing — AI Startup Growth Marketing Playbook

> Content, SEO, community, paid acquisition, and launch strategies from 0 → $10M.

---

## 1. Marketing Strategy by Stage

### Pre-Launch ($0 revenue)
- Build in public (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, indie hackers)
- Create waitlist with referral mechanics
- Write 5-10 SEO articles targeting pain points
- Engage in 3-5 communities where your ICP hangs out
- Launch on Product Hunt (aim for top 5 of the day)

### Early Stage ($0 → $100K ARR)
- **Content marketing**: 2-4 blog posts/week targeting long-tail keywords
- **Social proof**: Collect testimonials, case studies from early users
- **Community**: Start Discord/Slack community
- **Partnerships**: Cross-promote with complementary tools
- **Cold outreach**: Personalized emails to ICP

### Growth Stage ($100K → $1M ARR)
- **SEO**: Programmatic SEO for scale
- **Paid**: Google Ads on high-intent keywords, LinkedIn for B2B
- **Content**: YouTube tutorials, podcasts, webinars
- **Integrations**: Build integrations with popular tools (marketplace distribution)
- **Affiliate**: Launch affiliate/referral program

### Scale ($1M → $10M ARR)
- **Brand**: Invest in brand marketing and awareness
- **Events**: Sponsor/speak at industry events
- **Enterprise marketing**: Case studies, whitepapers, ROI calculators
- **Analyst relations**: Get featured in Gartner/G2/Capterra

## 3. SEO Strategy

### Technical SEO Checklist
- [ ] Site speed < 2s (Core Web Vitals passing)
- [ ] Mobile-responsive
- [ ] XML sitemap submitted
- [ ] robots.txt configured
- [ ] Structured data (JSON-LD)
- [ ] Internal linking strategy
- [ ] HTTPS everywhere
- [ ] Canonical URLs set

### Content SEO Framework
1. **Identify 50-100 target keywords** using Ahrefs
2. **Cluster into topic groups** (pillar + supporting articles)
3. **Create pillar pages** (2,000-4,000 words, comprehensive)
4. **Build supporting content** (targeting long-tail variations)
5. **Internal link** from supporting → pillar
6. **Update quarterly** to maintain rankings

### Programmatic SEO
- Create template pages for variations (e.g., "[tool] vs [competitor]", "[tool] for [industry]")
- Use AI to generate unique content for each variation
- Build comparison pages, alternative pages, landing pages at scale

## 4. Social Media Strategy

### Platform Priorities for B2B SaaS
| Platform | Priority | Strategy |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Twitter/X** | High | Build in public, engage with AI/tech community |
| **LinkedIn** | High | Thought leadership, B2B audience |
| **YouTube** | Medium | Tutorials, product demos, thought leadership |
| **Reddit** | Medium | Community engagement (not self-promotion) |
| **TikTok** | Low (unless B2C) | Short-form demos, behind-the-scenes |

### Content Calendar Template
- **Monday**: Educational thread (Twitter/LinkedIn)
- **Tuesday**: Product tip / feature highlight
- **Wednesday**: Industry insight / data point
- **Thursday**: Customer story / testimonial
- **Friday**: Behind-the-scenes / personal brand
- **Weekend**: Community engagement, replies

## 5. Email Marketing

### Email Sequences
1. **Welcome sequence** (5 emails over 7 days): Value delivery → activation
2. **Onboarding drip** (10 emails over 30 days): Feature education → upgrade
3. **Nurture sequence** (weekly): Content value → trust building
4. **Win-back sequence** (3 emails): Re-engage churned users
5. **Upgrade sequence** (3 emails): Power user → paid tier

### Email Metrics Benchmarks
| Metric | Good | Great |
|--------|------|-------|
| Open Rate | 25-35% | 35-50% |
| Click Rate | 3-5% | 5-10% |
| Unsubscribe Rate | < 0.5% | < 0.2% |
| Reply Rate | 1-3% | 3-5% |

## 6. Product Hunt Launch Playbook

### Pre-Launch (2-4 weeks before)
- Build a "ship" page on Product Hunt
- Recruit 200+ hunters who will upvote
- Prepare all assets (logo, screenshots, video, tagline)
- Schedule for Tuesday-Thursday launch

### Launch Day
- Post at 12:01 AM PST
- Respond to every comment within 15 minutes
- Share on all social channels
- Email your waitlist / users to engage
- Offer launch-day discount or bonus

### Post-Launch
- Follow up with all commenters
- Write a "lessons learned" blog post
- Leverage PH badge on website

## 7. Community-Led Growth

### Community Playbook
1. **Start small**: Discord or Slack with 20-50 active users
2. **Create rituals**: Weekly demos, AMAs, challenges
3. **Empower champions**: Identify and reward power users
4. **Content from community**: User stories, feature requests, bug reports
5. **Scale**: Community → user group → developer ecosystem

### Communities to Engage (AI/SaaS)
- Indie Hackers
- Hacker News
- Reddit: r/SaaS, r/startups, r/Entrepreneur
- Twitter/X AI community
- Discord: AI/ML communities
- LinkedIn groups
- Dev.to, Hashnode
